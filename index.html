How does Voiye help visually impaired individuals navigate public transportation?

Voiye utilizes advanced AI from the Gemini API, combined with text-to-speech technology and Google Maps Services, to provide precise audio guidance and time estimations for destinations. This ensures visually impaired users can navigate public transportation confidently and independently.

What is the Voiye Buddy system?

The Voiye Buddy system connects visually impaired users with volunteers who assist them during their travels. These buddies are incentivized through a gamified point system, where points can be converted into monetary rewards, encouraging consistent and high-quality support.

How can I become a Voiye Buddy?

Becoming a Voiye Buddy is easy! Simply sign up through the Voiye app, complete the registration process, and you’ll be ready to start assisting visually impaired users. As a buddy, you’ll earn points based on the distance you help cover, which can be redeemed for rewards.

How can organizations partner with Voiye?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that support accessibility initiatives. If you’re interested in partnering with us, please contact us through the "Partnerships" section on our website, and our team will get in touch with you to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.
